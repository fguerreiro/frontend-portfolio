About Me

I am currently based on Fortaleza, Brazil. I reach clients worldwide. The products of my work are known by their quality, maintainability and performance.

I am passionate about using creativity with logic to provide a real value for customers. My developer hat is usually of a .NET developer with python coming next. However, I like to secretly tell myself that I am a language and framework agnostic. That motivates me to try new hats when thinking about the solution for the next mission.

My Work
